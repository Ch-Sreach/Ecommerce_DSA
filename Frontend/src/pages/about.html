About Us

Passion for Fashion,
Rooted in Cambodia

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Phnom Penh, LUXE began as a dream: to bring premium, curated fashion to Cambodia. What started as a single rack in a small studio has grown into Southeast Asia's most trusted online fashion destination.

We believe getting dressed is an act of self-expression. Every piece in our collection is handpicked by our team of stylists who scour global fashion markets so you don't have to. We carry only what we love — and we love only quality.

Our mission is simple: make exceptional fashion accessible to everyone in Cambodia and beyond, delivered fast, wrapped beautifully, and backed by the best service in the business.

Our Values
